Privacy Policy (Draft)

BuellDocs is committed to protecting your privacy. This paystub generator operates entirely client-side in your browser. Data entered into the form is used solely for generating the paystub on your device and for the (simulated) order submission process described.

  • Data Entry: Information you enter is processed locally.
  • LocalStorage (Drafts): If you use the 'Save Draft' feature, form data is stored in your browser's LocalStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Order Submission: For order fulfillment (simulated), you will be asked to email specific details.
  • No Server-Side Storage of Paystub Data: The detailed paystub data generated by this tool is not stored on BuellDocs servers.

This policy will be updated with more details as BuellDocs services expand.
